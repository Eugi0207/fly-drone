QUALITY, DETAIL, PRIDE

Acacia Landscaping is a boutique landscape design and construction company with a goal of creating beautiful gardens that compliment and enhance the existing architecture and features of a home.

We work directly with clients, landscape designers, architects and builders to construct gardens that are not only aesthetically pleasing but functional so that families and individuals can enjoy their gardens and outdoor areas all year round.

Based in Ballarat, Victoria. Acacia Landscaping prides itself on delivering bespoke gardens with a balance between structural hardscapes, complimentary materials and horticulture.
